DECKSDEEP

Web Design, UX/UI, Figma

This max patch is for a light installation called Light Graffiti. The patch emits lights onto a wall, projecting shapes and text relating to rave/dance culture encouraging the audience to move and do what the text intructs them to do. The lights include one text saying "SHAKE" and two still frames of a woman dancing so when the light flashes and alternates, the frames imitiates a dancing motion. This installation is best suitable at a rave setting and I hope in the future I install more lights to cover the whole room.
